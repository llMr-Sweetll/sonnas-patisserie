# Brand & Marketing — Sonna's Patisserie

## Who we are
Sonna's Patisserie is Hubli's artisanal cake & dessert bakery — 100% vegetarian,
small-batch, made fresh every day. We are a real, local, twin-city (Hubli–Dharwad)
name with a 4.9★ reputation. We never describe ourselves as a "cloud kitchen" —
we are a *patisserie*, a place that takes cake seriously.

## The positioning: sell the problem, not the product
Steve-Jobs style — lead with the problem the customer didn't know they had.

**The problem:** most mass-market cakes hide cheap chemistry. Additives linked to
real harm are common in commercial bakery:
- **Potassium bromate** — IARC "possibly carcinogenic to humans," banned in India
  since 2016, yet still misused.
- **Azodicarbonamide, titanium dioxide, synthetic dyes** — banned in the EU over
  health concerns.
- **Trans fats / vanaspati (hydrogenated shortening)** — used instead of real butter.

**Our answer:** a Sonna's cake hides *nothing*. Real butter, couverture chocolate,
all-natural cocoa butter, real fruit for colour. 100% vegetarian, eggless on request.

**The line:** *"The cake you can trust to be worth it."* Indulgence you don't have
to second-guess. Royal on the outside, honest on the inside.

## Audience (Hubli–Dharwad)
~1.23M metro population, the commercial hub of North Karnataka, ~87% literacy — an
aspirational, family-oriented, celebration-driven market. We speak to pride in a
local name that meets a metro standard: *"Made for Hubli, by Hubli."* Occasions
(birthdays, anniversaries, festivals like Rakhi/Diwali) are the heartbeat.

## Voice
Warm, confident, a little playful — never clinical, never boastful. Short lines.
We explain *why* something matters (the health angle) without lecturing. Emojis are
welcome on WhatsApp, sparing on the website.

## Visual identity — "Blush & Antique Gold"
Boutique, regal, soft. Photography is the hero; the palette frames it.

| Token | Hex | Use |
|------|-----|-----|
| Blush | `#F6DDE0` | soft bands, tile base |
| Rose | `#B14A5E` | primary accent, CTAs, links |
| Deep rose | `#8E3A4B` | hovers |
| Antique gold | `#C7A24E` | hairlines, rules, secondary CTA |
| Ivory | `#F9F3EA` | page background |
| Cocoa ink | `#3A2A28` | text, dark "promise" band |

- **Type:** a serif display (Cormorant/Playfair stack) + a clean sans body (Inter).
- **Motifs:** the double-loop monogram; a thin gold hairline under section titles.
- **Veg mark** stays regulation green — it is a compliance symbol, not a brand colour.

Everything lives in `public/style.css` as CSS variables; change a token, not a rule.
