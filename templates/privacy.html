Privacy notice

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act). Last updated 5 July 2026.

What we collect, and why

When you place an order — on this site or on WhatsApp — we collect your name, phone number, delivery address, and optionally your email, for exactly one purpose: preparing and delivering your order and sending you updates about it (order confirmation and delivery status, on WhatsApp). If you write a message for a cake, we store that too, so we can pipe it on.

We do not run ads, we do not sell or share your data for marketing, we do not profile you, and we do not use analytics trackers or cookies beyond what the shop needs to work (your cart and, if you're staff, a login session).

Who else touches your data

  • Razorpay processes your payment. Your card/UPI details go directly to Razorpay — they never touch our servers.
  • Meta (WhatsApp) carries order conversations and updates on WhatsApp's infrastructure.
  • Supabase hosts our database in India-region data centres, encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • If you type a free-form message to our WhatsApp assistant, it may be processed by Anthropic's Claude to understand your request. Only the message text and our menu are shared — never payment details.

How long we keep it

Order records are kept for as long as tax and accounting law requires, then deleted. WhatsApp conversation state is kept only while your order is in progress.

Your rights

You may ask us, at any time, to show you the personal data we hold about you, correct it, or erase it (once no legal retention duty applies). We respond within 7 days.

Grievances

Contact our grievance officer on WhatsApp (+91 91132 31424) or at the shop: Sonna's Patisserie, Akshay Colony, Vidya Nagar, Hubli, Karnataka. If unsatisfied, you may escalate to the Data Protection Board of India.

Children

Our service is intended for adults. We do not knowingly process children's data and we do not do behavioural advertising of any kind.
