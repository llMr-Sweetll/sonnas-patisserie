Terms, delivery & refunds

Ordering

All items are made fresh to order and are 100% vegetarian. Prices are in Indian Rupees and include the product only; delivery within Hubli is arranged per order. An order is confirmed only when payment completes on Razorpay.

Delivery

We deliver within Hubli between 2pm and 10pm, in the window you pick at checkout. We are closed on Tuesdays. Please make sure someone is available at the address — cakes don't enjoy waiting in the sun.

Cancellations & refunds

  • Cancel up to 24 hours before your delivery date for a full refund — message us on WhatsApp with your order number.
  • Made-to-order items already in production cannot be cancelled.
  • If an order arrives damaged or wrong, tell us within 2 hours with a photo and we'll replace it or refund it, your choice.
  • Refunds go back to the original payment method within 5–7 working days via Razorpay.

Customization

Messages written on cakes are reproduced as you typed them — do give the spelling a second look. We may decline offensive text.
