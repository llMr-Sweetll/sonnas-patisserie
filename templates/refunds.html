Cancellation & Refunds

Last updated 6 July 2026.

Cancelling an order

  • You may cancel up to 24 hours before your chosen delivery date for a full refund — message us on WhatsApp or call +91 91132 31424 with your order number.
  • Because every cake is made fresh to order, cancellations within 24 hours of delivery cannot be accepted once the cake is in production.
  • Custom cakes with a personalised message or design are made specially for you and cannot be cancelled once baking has begun.

If something is wrong with your order

If your order arrives damaged, incorrect, or not as described, tell us within 2 hours of delivery with a photo, on WhatsApp. We'll arrange a replacement or a full refund — your choice.

Refund timelines

  • Approved refunds are initiated within 24–48 hours of confirmation.
  • The amount reaches your original payment method (UPI/card/net-banking via Razorpay) in 5–7 business days, depending on your bank.
  • Refunds are always made to the original payment method; we do not refund to a different account.

Questions

For anything about a cancellation or refund, contact us on WhatsApp +91 91132 31424 or email hello@sonnaspatisserie.in (2pm–10pm, closed Tuesdays). See also our Shipping & Delivery Policy and Terms.
