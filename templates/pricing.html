Pricing

Last updated 6 July 2026.

How our prices work

  • Every product's price is shown in Indian Rupees (₹) on its page and reflects the item as described (weight/size noted in the description).
  • Prices are inclusive of applicable taxes unless stated otherwise at checkout.
  • The price you see on the product page is the price you pay for that item — we re-confirm your full total on the checkout and payment screens before you pay.

What affects the total

  • Quantity and any eggless option you select.
  • Custom messages / personalisation on the cake (usually included; any add-on cost is shown before payment).
  • Delivery within Hubli, arranged per order — any charge is shown at checkout (see Shipping & Delivery).

Payments

Payments are processed securely by Razorpay (UPI, cards, net-banking, and wallets). Your card and banking details are handled by Razorpay and never stored on our servers. Your order is confirmed only once payment is completed.

Indicative price range

Single-serve desserts start from around ₹320; signature cakes typically range from ₹850 to ₹1,300. Bespoke and larger celebration cakes are quoted individually — message us on WhatsApp for a quote.
