Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last updated 6 July 2026.

Where we deliver

We deliver freshly baked cakes and desserts within Hubli (Hubballi), Karnataka. We do not currently ship outside the city or offer courier/postal delivery, because our products are perishable and made fresh for same-day enjoyment.

Delivery days & times

  • Deliveries run 2:00pm to 10:00pm, in the time window you choose at checkout.
  • We are closed on Tuesdays — no deliveries that day.
  • Orders are baked on the day of delivery; please place custom orders at least a day ahead where possible.

Delivery charges

Delivery within Hubli is arranged per order. Any applicable delivery charge is shown at checkout before you pay; see our Pricing page for how this works.

On the day

  • Please ensure someone is available at the delivery address during your chosen window — cakes are delicate and shouldn't wait in the heat.
  • We'll keep you updated on WhatsApp as your order is confirmed and sent out for delivery.
  • If we can't reach you or nobody is available, we'll contact you to reschedule; re-delivery may incur an additional charge.

Contact

Questions about a delivery? WhatsApp +91 91132 31424 or email hello@sonnaspatisserie.in. See also Cancellation & Refunds.
